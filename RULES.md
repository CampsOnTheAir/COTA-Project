# Proposed Rules

These rules are discussion points only.
Nothing here is final.


## Basic activation

• Operate from an eligible campsite
• Complete 15 QSOs
• Within a 48 hour period


## Eligible sites

Proposed:

- Provincial campgrounds
- National park campgrounds
- Crown recreation sites
- Municipal campgrounds

Potential future additions:

- Private campgrounds by recomendation/review
- Backcountry locations as a seperate catagory
