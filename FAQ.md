## Is this affiliated with POTA?

No.
COTA is a separate community project.

## Why another award program?

Because camping and portable radio naturally fit together.

## Can I help?

Absolutely.
Everything is currently under discussion.

## Is this only HF?

No.
One of the goals is to make VHF/UHF equally welcome.

## Is this replacing POTA?

No.
POTA and COTA have different focuses.
POTA celebrates operating from parks and protected areas.
COTA focuses on combining camping, outdoor recreation and amateur radio.
The goal is not to replace existing activities, but to create another reason for people to bring radios outdoors.
